Beef Stroganoff

Description

Taking the essentials flavors from a traditional beef stroganoff recipe, this version is simplied for the everyday chef but still makes for an excellent dinner. Taking a few shortcuts along the way to a flavorful meal, this recipe will taste great but won't require a long evening in the kitchen.


Ingredients

  • Egg noddles
  • Lean ground beef
  • Cream of mushroom soup
  • Garlic poweder
  • Sour Cream
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Drain fat from pot pan with meat, then stir in cream of mushroom and garlic powder
  4. Stir in noodles
  5. Enjoy!
