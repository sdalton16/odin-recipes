Lasagna

Description

Dubbed the "World's Best Lasanga Recipe" by allrecipes.com. This lasanga recipe has been on the site for 20+ years and has been one of the best performing recipes. While lasanga is time-consuming, it is a fan-favorite (and some may argue it is even better as leftovers).


Ingredients

  • Meat (lean ground beef or your favorite Italian sausage)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices
    • Parsley
    • Dried basil leaves
    • Salt
    • Italian seasoning
    • Fennel seeds
    • Black pepper
  • Lasanga noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Mix cheeses
  4. Mix tomato products into sauce
  5. Layer ingredients
    1. Noodles
    2. Meat
    3. Cheese mix
    4. Sauce
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese
  7. Cover with foil and bake at 375 for 50 minutes
  8. Allow to rest for at least 15 minutes or until cool enough to eat
  9. Enjoy!
